ABOUT

NSD-electronic Ltd. was founded in 2006, as the successor of the company SZR NSD-elektronik, founded in 1993 in the first place.


The company is engaged in process and machine automation, production, engineering, consulting and industrial services. We provide our customers with technical support, training, conceptual and "turnkey" solutions.
